# Weather-ARG
This ARG is tied to the weather

# Why the Weather?
well I'm old, and I focus way too much on the weather, and I started noticing patterns in my home state of NC
- the winter is tied to the summer
  - meaning however cold the winter gets, it will be that hot in the summer
- it's raining every day in November
- when it's raining in the summer, humidity comes and hits us, making it impossible to stand in the heat for that long
- fall and spring are really our main <em>"NORMAL"</em> months

# so what did I do?
Instead of having a normal thing that exists and making it only survivable during 2 seasons, I made it only 2 months
- October
- April

Each other month has a way to actually kill people in the ARG

# But how does this fit in an ARG?
simple, you'll be working under the weatherman
- a man that is trying to make people die and suffer
- if you fail him, no one will be saved, but if you cussed, you can warn all
  - but is the cost of millions really worth the sacrifices for information?
